## 📌 **Question**

The state capital of Brandenburg is called ...



---

### 📝 **Options:**

🔘 **A)** Potsdam.

🔘 **B)** Cottbus.

🔘 **C)** Brandenburg.

🔘 **D)** Frankfurt/Oder.

---

<details>
  <summary>🔍 Show Answer</summary>

  <p>
💡  <b>Correct Answer:</b>  a
  </p>
  <p>
    📖<b>Explanation:</b>
    Brandenburg is one of the 16 federal states of Germany and surrounds the capital Berlin. As a federal state, Brandenburg has several larger cities that play an important role in administration and culture. The state capital is the administrative center in which the state government has its seat. Important cities besides Potsdam are Cottbus and Frankfurt (Oder). The designation of the state capital is decisive for the political organization and administration of the federal state.
